[b]Extended Equipment Loadouts[/b] is Quality of Life mod that improves the vanilla loadout system.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
 [*] Up to three extra loadouts (configurable).
 [*] Items that let you copy accessories equipped in other loadouts.
[/list]

[b]Note:[/b] Make sure you remove all items from the loadout slots before disabling the mod! Loadout menus 4 through 6 are only accessible while this mod is enabled.

Special thanks to eugine1beetle for reporting multiple major bugs and contributing improvements!
